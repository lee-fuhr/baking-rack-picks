Best baking racks for 2026

Quick answer

The Oxo Good Grips Non-Stick Wire Cooling Rack is our top pick for its sturdy build, non-slip feet, and generous size. The Nordic Ware Natural Aluminum Commercial Cooling Rack is a close second for bakers who want an affordable, no-nonsense workhorse.

If you need multiple racks for batch baking, the Wilton set is a smart budget choice; for heavy-duty roasting or daily use, splurge on the Cuisinart.

A good baking rack does more than just hold cookies, it allows air to circulate under baked goods, preventing soggy bottoms and uneven cooling. The best racks are sturdy, rust-resistant, and have tall legs for proper airflow. Size matters too: a rack that fits your sheet pan without overhang will be most stable. When choosing a rack, consider the material (non-stick coated or bare aluminum/stainless steel), whether it’s dishwasher safe, and how many racks you need for your typical bake. Non-stick coating makes cleanup easier but can scratch; uncoated metal racks are more durable but may require hand washing. Feet height affects airflow and stability, so look for at least half-inch legs.
